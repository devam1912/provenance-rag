3. Categorization of Offers
3.1 Types of Recruitment Offers Campus processes facilitate four distinct types of placements:
1. Job (J) Only: Available exclusively to final-year students. 2. Winter Internship + Job (WI + J): Available exclusively to final-year students. 3. Winter Internship (WI) Only: Available exclusively to final-year students. 4. Summer Internship (SI): Available exclusively to pre-final year students. 2

3.2 Categorization of Companies Recruiting firms are segmented strictly by Cost to Company (CTC):
●

Category I: Firms offering a package of INR 10 lakhs per annum (LPA) and above .
●

Category II: Firms offering a package below INR 10 lakhs per annum (LPA) . Important CTC Valuation Clauses:
●

Packages are determined uniformly via the gross Cost to Company (CTC).
●

In instances where a company defines a salary range for WI+J or J-only offers, the lower bound

of

the

range

dictates

the

corporate

category

and

switch

eligibility

(e.g.,

a

range

of

9.00

to

11.00

LPA

is

fixed

at

9.00

LPA).

●

For WI-only offers, the category is determined using the projected full-time employment (FTE)

conversion

CTC

declared

by

the

company.

●

If a company adjusts its compensation package mid-drive, altering its tier designation, the

student's

status

and

subsequent

policy

constraints

will

be

retroactively

adjusted

to

match

the

new

category.
